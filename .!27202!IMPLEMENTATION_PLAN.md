- `POST /api/v1/calculations/iron-steel/calculate`
- `POST /api/v1/calculations/{sector}/validate` (each sector)
- `POST /api/v1/calculations/{sector}/export` (each sector)
- `GET /api/v1/factors?sector=cement|oil_gas|pulp_paper`

- `GET /api/v1/templates/activity`
